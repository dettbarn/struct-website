Biography

Struct, experimental semi-melodic noise music project from southern Germany.

Founded somewhen between 2008 and 2016.

influenced by Nine Inch Nails, Aphex Twin, Skinny Puppy, Merzbow, Sunn O))), and many others.

Do not hope for radio-friendliness.
